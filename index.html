First Week Progress

Awesome Inc's Bootcamp has begun, Aug 27, 2020
"Volumes 12-24 of Git Documentation"

A great first week!

After being excited about this program for a while, I was happy to jump into the work right away. From what I had heard, I anticipated a large amount of reading, and so far it's to the level that I expected. I imagined that there would be an enormous amount to cover in just 16 weeks, and I have a feeling that it's only going to get harder from here. But I know that if I keep at it every day and do my best to challenge myself, then the effort is sure to pay off.

Right now, my roadblock might be getting backgrounds and images to work the way I want them. On this first draft of the blog, I had some trouble getting a nice background image to function with these article blocks, but I couldn't quite make it work - you might see some unsightly seams along the sides from my clumsy attempt. I'm glad I made the effort to see whether I could do it however, and am eager to learn more in the next week and maybe push this blog a little bit further!
